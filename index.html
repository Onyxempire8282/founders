A Letter from the Founder

I didn't build Claim Cipher because I wanted to start a software company.

I built it because managing high-volume claims across multiple firms without burning out requires more than hustle — it requires systems.

Over the years, I've worked with a growing number of TPAs. The work came, the volume came, and eventually the realization came too: Scaling solo has a ceiling.

Routes stack. Admin multiplies. Total loss paperwork compounds. And at some point, the friction costs more than the work pays.

Claim Cipher began as internal tools — mileage tracking, routing logic, workflow organization — built to make the day-to-day manageable. Not polished. Not marketed. Just functional.

What became clear over time was this: If the work is going to grow, the system has to grow with it.

Claim Cipher is designed to help adjusters and appraisers:

  • Manage multiple firms without chaos
  • Reduce admin enough to delegate responsibly
  • Bring spouses or family members into the business in real ways
  • Build sustainability instead of constant recovery

This isn't an all-in-one platform. It's a modular survival suite built by someone still in the work.

I shared this first with a private community that played a real role in my growth. I'm sharing it here now because I know many of you are navigating the same pressures — volume, responsibility, and the desire to build something that lasts.
